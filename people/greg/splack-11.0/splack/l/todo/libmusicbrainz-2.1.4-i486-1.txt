libmusicbrainz: libmusicbrainz (library for accessing MusicBrainz servers)
libmusicbrainz:
libmusicbrainz: MusicBrainz is a user-maintained community music metadatabase.
libmusicbrainz: The MusicBrainz client library allows applications to make metadata
libmusicbrainz: lookups to a MusicBrainz server, generate signatures from WAV files
libmusicbrainz: and create CD Index Disk IDs from audio CD-ROMs.
